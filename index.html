Serving San Francisco and the peninsula. Trail Hikes for active dogs.

PACK WALK

A one-hour nature hike 3-5 days a week. Your pal will enjoy an active outing in the fresh air, rain or shine. He or she will hit the rugged trails of the Bay Area with one of our Pack Hikers and a group of regular doggy friends.

$35 per walk

SOLO WALK

Does your pooch need a little help getting used to the sights and sounds of the city? Or maybe they need to get away from the city to feel the grass under their paws and sniff to their heart’s content out in nature? We can help! A one-hour Solo Walk with one of our Pack Hikers is specifically designed for sensitive pups who may not like walking with a group, or need some extra space to feel comfortable.

$60 per walk

DOG FU 101

Dog Fu is ability to tap into the unbreakable symbiotic bond between man and beast. Do you have questions for our experts? Need some help with Dog-Fu or leash manners? This option is ideal for new pups who are still trying to figure out the world or dogs who struggle with their p’s and q’s on leash. You and your bud will take a 90-minute walk with one of our knowledgable Sensei to assess and strategize solutions for rude doggie behavior.

$85 per session

HOUSE CALL

We’ll work on Canine Courtesy in your home or workplace. Expert eyeballs on your dogs living situation will identify any invisible stressors and make sure Fido is comfortable and thriving wherever he has to spend time.

$125 per visit

JIM PATT

FOUNDER & TRAIL MASTER

A San Francisco native and former motorcycle mechanic turned techie turned dog professional, Jim has a long history of figuring out how things work and what makes them tick. He’s been a professional dog walker for 3 years, and a dog dad for 6. Adventure Dog’s philosophy can be traced back to Jim’s innate curiosity and analytic nature - he strives to understand the whys of dog behavior, and then applies science and common sense to help build better relationships between dogs and their owners. Communication is key to any successful human-dog partnership, and Jim helps humans speak a language their dog can understand.

LEEJAY VICTOR

CREATIVE & OPERATIONS

LeeJay has made a career out of wrangling creatives and refining workflow and design processes for a number of tech companies in the Bay Area. She likes to build things and solve problems. An organizer at her core, LeeJay seeks order in chaos, and patterns in seeming randomness. Applying these principles to dog behavior has helped bolster Adventure Dog’s scientific approach to healthy and happy human-dog communication. LeeJay has years of hands-on experience with strong breeds and “special needs” dogs - she has a soft spot for the dogs that struggle to fit in and function in what can sometimes be a rather overwhelming world.

BARNEY

CHIEF NAPTIME OFFICER

Barney is Jim’s stalwart companion and trusty sidekick. He’s a trooper who will hike miles and miles on the toughest trails and still perk up if there’s even the slightest chance of going back O-U-T-S-I-D-E. If he’s not out on the trail, or playing fetch at the beach, Barney can always be found snuggled up in the coziest spot in the house, snuffling and snorting away in dreamland, chasing imaginary squirrels, and probably not catching them, just like in real life.

MALCOM

ORIGINAL ADVENTURE DOG

Mal is the latest addition to the pack. He is LeeJay’s unintentional rescue pup, the (giant) little brother Barney never wanted, and the overgrown puppy that’s still working hard on his manners. He’s an energetic pooch who thrives on a ton of activity and engagement, but he missed out on socialization and, well, just learning how to be a dog. He is curious and playful and a never-ending fount of energy. Jim and LeeJay both realized the ability to spend time outdoors on daily hikes with the dogs was a privilege and a luxury - something all dogs deserved, but not all humans could give their canine companions every day. And thus Adventure Dog came to be.
