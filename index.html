Serving San Francisco and the peninsula. Trail Hikes for active dogs.

PACK WALK

A one-hour nature hike 3-5 days a week. Your pal will enjoy an active outing in the fresh air, rain or shine. He or she will hit the rugged trails of the Bay Area with one of our Pack Hikers and a group of regular doggy friends.

$35 per walk

SOLO WALK

Does your pooch need a little help getting used to the sights and sounds of the city? Or maybe they need to get away from the city to feel the grass under their paws and sniff to their heart’s content out in nature? We can help! A one-hour Solo Walk with one of our Pack Hikers is specifically designed for sensitive pups who may not like walking with a group, or need some extra space to feel comfortable.

$60 per walk

DOG FU 101

Dog Fu is ability to tap into the unbreakable symbiotic bond between man and beast. Do you have questions for our experts? Need some help with Dog-Fu or leash manners? This option is ideal for new pups who are still trying to figure out the world or dogs who struggle with their p’s and q’s on leash. You and your bud will take a 90-minute walk with one of our knowledgable Sensei to assess and strategize solutions for rude doggie behavior.

$85 per session

HOUSE CALL

We’ll work on Canine Courtesy in your home or workplace. Expert eyeballs on your dogs living situation will identify any invisible stressors and make sure Fido is comfortable and thriving wherever he has to spend time.

$125 per visit
